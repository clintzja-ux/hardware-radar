How We Choose

Every recommendation follows a simple process built on value and transparency.

Understanding how a recommendation is made is just as important as the recommendation itself.

We Start With Price

We regularly compare prices from trusted online retailers.

Prices change throughout the day, so our recommendations use the most recent verified pricing available.

We Verify the Product

Price alone is not enough.

We verify the product specifications to make sure you are comparing the correct memory type, capacity, speed, and retailer listing.

We Recommend Value

Hardware Radar answers the cheapest question first.

When spending a little more better serves a specific need, such as gaming, RGB, workstation use, or a laptop upgrade, we show that option separately.

We Show Alternatives

Different buyers have different priorities.

We show the next-lowest verified prices and a small number of useful alternatives without overwhelming you with unnecessary choices.

Recommendations Are Earned

Products earn their place through:

  • Verified pricing
  • Value
  • Suitability

Rankings are not sold to retailers or advertisers.

Transparency builds trust. That is why we explain how every recommendation is made.
