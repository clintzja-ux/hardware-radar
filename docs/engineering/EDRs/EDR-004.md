EDR-004

Title

Validators Execute Rules; They Do Not Understand Them

Decision

Validators are execution engines only.

They:

iterate rules,
execute validation functions,
normalize results,
handle exceptions.

They never interpret product-specific business logic.

Reason

This preserves complete separation between:

engine behavior
domain knowledge

As a result, adding support for a new product family requires only:

a schema,
a rule set,
validator functions,

without modifying the Sentinel engine itself.

I think this is one of the most important architectural decisions we've made because it protects the engine from accumulating business-specific logic over time.
