
# Hardware Radar Product Vision & Strategy
**Version:** 1.0 (Foundational)
**Owner:** Mirabelle Labs

# Executive Vision

Hardware Radar exists to help people make confident hardware buying decisions through accurate technical knowledge, trustworthy recommendations, and current market intelligence.

Our long-term ambition is not to become the largest hardware affiliate website.

Our ambition is to become **the world's most trustworthy hardware intelligence platform.**

---

# Mission

Help users answer three questions:

1. What is this hardware?
2. Is it a good choice?
3. Where can I buy it today at a fair price?

---

# Core Principles

- Truth before price.
- Trust before revenue.
- Compliance by design.
- Explain recommendations.
- Separate facts from opinions.
- Every recommendation should be traceable.

# Engineering Principles

Every public page must be traceable to:

- Atlas revision
- Mercury observation
- Sentinel validation
- Forge publication event

No undocumented shortcuts.

---

# Decision Filter

Before adding a feature ask:

1. Does it improve trust?
2. Does it improve buying decisions?
3. Does it fit the architecture?
4. Can it be maintained?
5. Is it worth delaying the MVP?

If "no" to most questions, defer it.
